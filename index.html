About the tournament

A backyard rivalry, with a trophy

The Golden Pickle brings Andrews together for a day of doubles pickleball — equal parts competition and community cookout. Whether you're a 3.0 weekend warrior or you've been dinking since before it was cool, there's a bracket for you. Grab a partner, pick your division, and come find out who takes home the Golden Pickle.

How it works

Three steps to the kitchen line

Step One

Sign up your team

Register you and your doubles partner on the sign-up page. Pick your skill level and lock your spot before the field fills up.

Step Two

Watch the bracket

Once the field is set, your matchups, courts, and start times will be posted on the schedule & bracket page.

Step Three

Play for gold

Show up on July 11th, play your matches, and chase the Golden Pickle. Final standings go up on the results page.
