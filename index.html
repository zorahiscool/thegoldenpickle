About the tournament

A fun game, with a trophy

The Golden Pickle brings Andrews together for a day of doubles pickleball — equal parts competition and fun. Whether you're a weekend warrior or you've been playing for a while, there's a spot for you. Grab a partner,and come find out who takes home the Golden Pickle.
